## 🎯 The Problem

According to **FluentCap**, **54% of employees face language barriers at work**, and distributed teams lose up to **25% of their productivity** due to miscommunication. The rise of remote jobs has enabled companies to hire talented people from around the world, but many foreigner employees struggle to communicate effectively during video calls, meetings, and collaborative sessions.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**Scarlet's Thread** is an intelligent real-time translation system powered by **Google Gemini API 2.5 Flash**. Our AI assistant, **Scarlet**, enables seamless communication between people who speak different languages—currently supporting **English to Spanish translation**.

### What Scarlet Does:
- 🎙️ **Real-time Audio Processing**: Listens to what the other person says during calls
- 🌐 **Instant Translation**: Translates English speech to Spanish subtitles
- 💬 **Smart Suggestions**: Proposes natural, professional responses in English
- 📚 **Grammar Tips**: Provides quick linguistic insights for language learners
- 🔴 **Live Overlay**: Displays translations directly on your screen (Google Meet-ready)
